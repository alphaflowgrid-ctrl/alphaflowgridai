## Free AI Contract Review Checklist for Legal Teams

AI can help legal teams review contracts faster. It can summarize long agreements, identify risky clauses, suggest redlines, compare language against playbooks, and draft questions for negotiation.

But contract review is high-stakes work.

AI should support legal judgment, not replace it. Every AI-generated summary, risk flag, or redline should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before it is used with a client, counterparty, or internal business team.

This guide gives you a practical AI contract review checklist, plus prompts you can use for first-pass review, clause analysis, negotiation notes, and legal workflow improvement.

## AI Contract Review Flow

```text
Upload or paste contract text
        ↓
Ask AI for plain-English summary
        ↓
Identify key clauses and obligations
        ↓
Compare against internal playbook
        ↓
Flag risks and missing terms
        ↓
Draft negotiation questions
        ↓
Human legal review
        ↓
Finalize redlines or advice
```

The most important step is the human legal review.

AI can assist, but legal responsibility stays with the lawyer or legal team.

## Contract Review Checklist

| Step | What to Check | Why It Matters |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Parties and legal names | Wrong parties can create enforceability issues |
| 2 | Effective date and term | Determines when obligations begin and end |
| 3 | Payment terms | Impacts cash flow, late fees and disputes |
| 4 | Termination rights | Defines how either side can exit the agreement |
| 5 | Liability limits | Controls financial exposure |
| 6 | Indemnity language | Determines who covers third-party claims |
| 7 | Confidentiality | Protects sensitive business information |
| 8 | Data protection | Important for privacy, security and compliance |
| 9 | Governing law | Affects dispute handling and interpretation |
| 10 | Missing clauses | Helps catch gaps before signature |

## 7 AI Prompts for Contract Review

### 1. Contract Summary Prompt

```text
Act as a legal contract reviewer.

Summarize this contract in plain English.

Include:
- parties
- purpose of agreement
- key obligations
- payment terms
- contract term
- termination rights
- major risks

Contract:
[paste contract text]
```

Use this for a first-pass understanding of a long agreement.

---

### 2. Risk Flag Prompt

```text
Review this contract for business and legal risk.

Focus on:
- liability
- indemnity
- termination
- payment terms
- confidentiality
- data protection
- unusual obligations

Return risks in a table with:
- clause
- issue
- why it matters
- suggested follow-up question

Contract:
[paste contract text]
```

Use this before drafting redlines or asking business stakeholders for input.

---

### 3. Missing Clause Prompt

```text
Review this agreement and identify any missing clauses commonly expected in a [type of agreement].

Agreement type: [NDA / MSA / SaaS agreement / vendor contract / employment agreement]

Return:
- missing clause
- why it may matter
- whether it is critical or optional

Contract:
[paste contract text]
```

Use this when reviewing templates or counterparty drafts.

---

### 4. Clause Rewrite Prompt

```text
Rewrite this clause to be more favorable to [buyer/seller/customer/vendor].

Clause:
[paste clause]

Requirements:
- keep the language professional
- avoid aggressive wording
- explain what changed
- provide a fallback position
```

Use this to create negotiation-ready language.

---

### 5. Playbook Comparison Prompt

```text
Compare this clause against our preferred position.

Preferred position:
[paste playbook rule]

Contract clause:
[paste clause]

Return:
- matches playbook? yes/no/partial
- key differences
- risk level
- suggested redline
```

Use this if your legal team has an internal review playbook.

---

### 6. Negotiation Questions Prompt

```text
Create negotiation questions for this contract.

Focus on unclear, risky, or one-sided terms.

Return 10 questions grouped by:
- commercial terms
- legal risk
- data/security
- termination
- liability

Contract:
[paste contract text]
```

Use this before a negotiation call.

---

### 7. Business-Friendly Summary Prompt

```text
Summarize this contract for a non-lawyer business stakeholder.

Keep it short and practical.

Include:
- what we are agreeing to
- key deadlines
- payment or renewal terms
- top 3 risks
- decisions needed from the business team

Contract:
[paste contract text]
```

Use this when legal needs business input before approval.

## Final Recommendation

AI can speed up contract review, but it cannot take legal responsibility.

Use AI for summaries, first-pass risk flags, clause comparisons and negotiation prep. Then have a legal professional review the output before relying on it.

For Word-based drafting, use [Spellbook](/ai-tools/spellbook/).

For structured playbook review, use [LegalOn](/ai-tools/legalon/).

For full contract workflows, use [Ironclad](/ai-tools/ironclad/).

The safest legal AI workflow is not automatic approval. It is faster review with clear human oversight.
