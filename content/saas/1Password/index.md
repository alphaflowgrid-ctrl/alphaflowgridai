## What Is 1Password?

1Password is a password manager for individuals, families and teams. It stores passwords, passkeys, secure notes, payment details and sensitive business data in encrypted vaults, with sharing controls and security alerts.

## Honest Review

1Password is one of the most polished password managers. It is easy to use, secure by design and strong for teams that need to share credentials without emailing passwords around. Business features like SSO, provisioning and Watchtower make it useful beyond personal password storage.

The trade-off is that there is no permanent free plan. Bitwarden is a better choice for users who want free password management. For teams and families that value usability and security, 1Password is excellent.
