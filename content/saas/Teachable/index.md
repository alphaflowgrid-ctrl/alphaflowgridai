## What Is Teachable?

Teachable is a platform for selling online courses, coaching, digital downloads and education products. It includes course hosting, payments, student apps, certificates, upsells, affiliate tools, global payments and tax handling.

## Honest Review

Teachable is one of the easiest ways for creators to start selling courses. It handles the technical side of course hosting, checkout, payments and student access so creators can focus on content and marketing.

The trade-off is pricing and transaction fees. Starter is approachable but includes a 7.5% transaction fee. Serious creators should usually consider Builder or Growth. Teachable is strong for selling education products, but less flexible than a fully custom LMS.
