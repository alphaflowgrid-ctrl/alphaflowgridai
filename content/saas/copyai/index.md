## What Is Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is an AI writing tool focused on sales and marketing copy. It specialises in short-form content — email sequences, ad copy, landing pages, social media posts, and product descriptions. It includes pre-built workflows for common marketing tasks, making it fast to produce repetitive content at scale.

## Honest Review

Copy.ai is genuinely useful for sales and marketing teams that produce high volumes of short-form copy. The free tier at 2,000 words/month is enough to evaluate whether it fits your workflow. Note that Copy.ai raised its pricing in 2026 — the entry paid **Starter** plan is now **$49/month** (~$36 annual), with **Pro at ~$99** and **Team at ~$249**. For pure writing quality, Claude Pro at $20/month produces better output at a far lower price — Copy.ai's real value is its marketing-specific templates and workflow automation, not raw writing quality.
