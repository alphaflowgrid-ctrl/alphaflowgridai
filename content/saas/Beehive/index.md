## What Is Beehiiv?

Beehiiv is a newsletter platform for creators, publishers and media brands. It helps users write newsletters, host a website, grow subscribers, sell paid subscriptions, run ads, use referrals and monetize an audience.

## Honest Review

Beehiiv is excellent for newsletter-first creators. The free Launch plan is generous, and paid plans unlock the growth tools creators actually want: referrals, ads, boosts, surveys, automations, digital products and branding control.

The main limitation is that Beehiiv is not a CRM or ecommerce automation platform. It is best for audience and media businesses, not complex sales pipelines or product-store marketing.
