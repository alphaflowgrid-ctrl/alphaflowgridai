## What Is Follow Up Boss?

Follow Up Boss is a real estate CRM for agents, teams and brokerages. It helps organize leads, automate follow-up, route inquiries, call, text, email, track performance and coach agents from one platform.

## Honest Review

Follow Up Boss is built around speed-to-lead. Real estate agents lose deals when leads are not contacted quickly, and FUB helps teams stay organized with smart lists, action plans, lead distribution and calling workflows.

The downside is cost for smaller agents. It is powerful if you have enough lead volume, but overkill if you only manage a few contacts. For teams and brokerages, the reporting, coaching and AI features make more sense.
