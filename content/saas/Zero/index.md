## What Is Xero?

Xero is cloud accounting software for small businesses. It helps users send invoices, track bills, reconcile bank transactions, manage sales tax, view reports, forecast cash flow and collaborate with accountants.

## Honest Review

Xero is a clean and modern accounting tool. It is especially strong for businesses that want bank reconciliation, real-time reports and simple online accounting without the clutter some users feel in QuickBooks.

The trade-off is ecosystem familiarity. In the US, many accountants know QuickBooks better, so Xero can require a little more accountant alignment. For businesses that like Xero’s interface and reporting, it is a strong accounting option.
