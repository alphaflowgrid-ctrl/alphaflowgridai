## What Is ActiveCampaign?

ActiveCampaign is an email marketing, CRM and automation platform for businesses that want behavior-based customer journeys. It helps teams send campaigns, segment contacts, automate follow-ups, track customer behavior, integrate ecommerce data and use AI-powered tools to build smarter workflows.

## Honest Review

ActiveCampaign is strongest when automation depth matters. The workflow builder, segmentation, tagging and behavioral triggers make it a powerful choice for lifecycle marketing, ecommerce retention, B2B nurturing and customer follow-up. Compared with simpler newsletter tools, ActiveCampaign gives marketers more control over who receives what message and when.

The trade-off is complexity and scaling cost. New users may need time to understand tags, lists, segments and automations. Pricing also increases as your contact list grows, and some features require higher plans or add-ons. If you want advanced automation, ActiveCampaign is strong. If you only need simple email broadcasts, MailerLite, Brevo or Moosend may be easier and cheaper.
