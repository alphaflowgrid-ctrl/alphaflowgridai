## What Is Clio?

Clio is cloud-based legal practice management software for law firms. It helps lawyers manage matters, contacts, calendars, documents, tasks, time tracking, billing, payments, client communication and legal workflows.

## Honest Review

Clio is one of the strongest legal practice management platforms because it covers the business side of running a law firm. It is especially useful for firms that need matter management, billing, document storage, client portals and reporting in one system.

The trade-off is add-on complexity. The base platform is strong, but many advanced capabilities like intake CRM, document automation, accounting and Manage AI are add-ons or higher-tier features. For growing law firms, Clio is excellent. For simple contract drafting, Spellbook or LegalOn may fit better.
