## What Is Klaviyo?

Klaviyo is an email, SMS and customer-data marketing platform built for ecommerce stores. It helps brands segment customers, automate abandoned cart flows, send campaigns, personalize product recommendations and track revenue from email and SMS.

## Honest Review

Klaviyo is one of the strongest marketing platforms for ecommerce because it understands purchase data deeply. Shopify, WooCommerce and BigCommerce stores can use it to create advanced flows based on browsing, buying, abandonment and lifecycle behavior.

The trade-off is cost and complexity. Klaviyo can become expensive as your profile count grows, and advanced segmentation takes time to master. It is excellent for revenue-focused ecommerce teams, but not ideal for simple newsletters.
