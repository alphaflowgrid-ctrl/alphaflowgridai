## What Is Omnisend?

Omnisend is an ecommerce marketing platform for email, SMS and web push campaigns. It helps online stores capture contacts, automate abandoned cart flows, personalize product recommendations and track campaign performance from one dashboard.

## Honest Review

Omnisend is one of the best-value email/SMS platforms for ecommerce. It includes a generous free plan, ecommerce automations, segmentation, AI forms and 24/7 support even for free users. That makes it easier to start than many competitors.

The main limitation is category focus. Omnisend is built for online stores, not general B2B marketing or creator newsletters. If you run ecommerce, it is excellent. If you need complex CRM or non-store workflows, ActiveCampaign or HubSpot may fit better.
