## What Is Descript?

Descript is an AI-powered audio and video editor that works differently from traditional editors — you edit the transcript and the video edits itself. It includes AI features like filler word removal, background noise reduction, eye contact correction, and AI voice cloning so you can fix mistakes by typing new words in your own voice.

## Honest Review

Descript is one of the most innovative tools for content creators in 2026. The concept of editing video by editing text is genuinely transformative for podcasters and YouTubers. The free tier includes 1 hour of transcription (720p, watermarked) — a great way to evaluate the product. Creator at $35/month ($24 annual) is the right plan for active content creators — the unlimited AI actions and voice features save hours of re-recording. Note that Descript moved to a media-minutes / AI-credits model in late 2025, so heavy uploads or AI use can require top-ups. The main limitation is it is not a professional colour-grading or effects tool.
