## What Is Descript?
Descript is an AI-powered audio and video editor that works differently from traditional editors — you edit the transcript and the video edits itself. It includes AI features like filler word removal, background noise reduction, eye contact correction, and AI voice cloning so you can fix mistakes by typing new words in your own voice.

## Honest Review
Descript is one of the most innovative tools for content creators in 2026. The concept of editing video by editing text is genuinely transformative for podcasters and YouTubers. The free tier includes 1 hour of transcription — a great way to evaluate the product. Creator at $40/month is the right plan for active content creators — the AI voice cloning feature alone saves hours of re-recording. The main limitation is it is not a professional colour grading or effects tool.
