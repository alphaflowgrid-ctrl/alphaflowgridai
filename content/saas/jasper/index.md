## What Is Jasper AI?
Jasper is an AI content platform designed specifically for marketing teams. It offers brand voice training, reusable templates, campaign management, and integrations with marketing tools. Jasper is built for consistency at scale — ensuring every piece of content sounds like your brand regardless of who writes it.

## Honest Review
Jasper is the right tool for marketing teams that need brand voice consistency across multiple writers. It is not the right tool for individuals — Claude Pro at $20/month is significantly cheaper and produces better writing quality. The Creator plan at $49/month is expensive for solo users. Where Jasper shines is team workflows — the brand voice training, shared templates, and campaign features justify the cost for marketing departments of 3 or more people.
