## What Is Thinkific?

Thinkific is a platform for creating and selling online courses, communities, memberships, digital downloads and coaching products. It helps creators build course websites, manage students, sell products and deliver learning experiences.

## Honest Review

Thinkific is a strong course platform for creators who want structured learning products. Unlimited courses on Basic, communities, downloads and sales tools make it useful for educators building a real course business.

The trade-off is that Thinkific is not a full marketing suite. You may still need separate email marketing, CRM or funnel tools. It is best when your main product is education content, not when newsletters or email automation are the core business.
