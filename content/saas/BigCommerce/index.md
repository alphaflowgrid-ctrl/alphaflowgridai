## What Is BigCommerce?

BigCommerce is a hosted ecommerce platform for building online stores, managing products, accepting payments and selling across channels. It is known for built-in ecommerce features, B2B capabilities, APIs and no platform transaction fees.

## Honest Review

BigCommerce is a strong Shopify alternative for businesses that want more native ecommerce features and no platform transaction fees. It is especially attractive for brands with B2B needs, larger catalogs or more complex commerce requirements.

The downside is usability. BigCommerce is powerful, but the admin experience can feel less beginner-friendly than Shopify. Its GMV-based plan limits can also create surprise upgrades as revenue grows.
