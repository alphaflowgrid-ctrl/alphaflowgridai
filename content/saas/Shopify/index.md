## What Is Shopify?

Shopify is an ecommerce platform for building online stores, accepting payments, managing inventory, selling across channels and growing a retail business. It includes hosting, checkout, themes, analytics, shipping tools, POS options and AI commerce features.

## Honest Review

Shopify is the easiest serious ecommerce platform for most merchants. The setup is simple, the checkout is strong, and the app ecosystem is huge. It works well for solo entrepreneurs, DTC brands, dropshippers, retail stores and larger commerce teams.

The trade-off is cost creep. Shopify’s base plans are clear, but apps, paid themes, transaction fees and POS add-ons can raise the real monthly cost. Still, for most ecommerce businesses, Shopify’s reliability and checkout quality justify the price.
