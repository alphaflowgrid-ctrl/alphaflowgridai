## What Is Gorgias?

Gorgias is an ecommerce customer support platform for helpdesk ticketing, live chat, social channels and AI agents. It connects deeply with Shopify, BigCommerce and ecommerce apps so support teams can resolve order questions faster.

## Honest Review

Gorgias is best when support tickets require ecommerce actions. Agents can view orders, edit subscriptions, handle refunds and answer pre-sales questions from one place. The AI Agent can automate repetitive inquiries like order status, returns and common FAQs.

The trade-off is pricing. Ticket-volume pricing and AI resolution fees can become expensive for high-volume stores. For Shopify-native brands with meaningful support volume, Gorgias is strong. For smaller stores, Tidio or Re:amaze may be easier to justify.
