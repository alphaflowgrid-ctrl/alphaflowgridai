## What Is FreshBooks?

FreshBooks is accounting and invoicing software for freelancers, contractors and service businesses. It helps users send invoices, track expenses, accept payments, create estimates, manage clients and generate basic financial reports.

## Honest Review

FreshBooks is best for people who bill clients. The interface is simple, invoices look professional and the workflow feels easier than full accounting systems. Freelancers, consultants and service businesses can manage most day-to-day finances without getting overwhelmed.

The trade-off is depth. FreshBooks is not as powerful as QuickBooks or Xero for complex accounting, inventory or larger finance teams. It is ideal for client-based businesses, not product-heavy ecommerce operations.
