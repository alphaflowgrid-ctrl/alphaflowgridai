## What Is BambooHR?

BambooHR is HR software for managing employee records, hiring, onboarding, time off, benefits, employee experience, performance and HR reporting. It gives HR teams one system for core people data and workflows.

## Honest Review

BambooHR is a strong HRIS for small and mid-sized companies. It is easier to adopt than many enterprise HCM tools and covers the core HR workflows most growing companies need: records, onboarding, time off, reporting, hiring and employee experience.

The trade-off is add-on cost. Payroll, benefits, time tracking and EOR features can add to the base plan. For SMBs wanting clean HR operations, BambooHR is excellent. For global enterprise HCM, Workday or SAP may be better.
