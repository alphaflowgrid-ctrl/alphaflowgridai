## QuickBooks vs Xero — Which Should You Choose?

QuickBooks and Xero are both strong accounting platforms. QuickBooks is more dominant in the US. Xero is cleaner and popular with cloud-first businesses.

## When To Choose QuickBooks

Choose QuickBooks if you want broad accounting features, accountant familiarity, AI bookkeeping and a large ecosystem.

## When To Choose Xero

Choose Xero if you prefer a cleaner interface and straightforward cloud accounting.

## Bottom Line

QuickBooks is the safer default for most US small businesses. Xero is a strong cleaner alternative.
