## Alteryx vs Datarails — Which Should You Choose?

Choose Alteryx if analysts need repeatable data prep, blending, transformations and automation across many business functions.

## When To Choose Datarails

Alteryx is better for analytics automation. Datarails is better for finance and FP&A workflows.
