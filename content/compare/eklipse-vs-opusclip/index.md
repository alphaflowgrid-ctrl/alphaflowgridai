## Eklipse vs OpusClip — Which Should You Choose?

Both tools create short clips, but they are built for different types of content.

## When To Choose Eklipse

Choose Eklipse if you stream games and want AI to find highlight moments from Twitch, YouTube or Kick.

## When To Choose OpusClip

Choose OpusClip if you repurpose podcasts, interviews, webinars or talking-head videos into short clips.

## Bottom Line

Eklipse is better for gaming. OpusClip is better for general long-form content.
