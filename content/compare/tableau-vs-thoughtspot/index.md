## Tableau vs ThoughtSpot — Which Should You Choose?

Tableau and ThoughtSpot both help teams analyze data, but they approach analytics differently.

## When To Choose Tableau

Choose Tableau if your organization wants rich dashboards, visual exploration and polished BI experiences.

## When To Choose ThoughtSpot

Choose ThoughtSpot if business users want to ask data questions in natural language and get AI-assisted insights.

## Bottom Line

Tableau is better for visual dashboards. ThoughtSpot is better for search-driven AI analytics.
