## Gorgias vs Tidio — Which Should You Choose?

Both tools support ecommerce customer conversations, but they serve different levels of complexity.

## When To Choose Gorgias

Choose Gorgias if your team handles many order-related Shopify tickets and needs deep ecommerce actions.

## When To Choose Tidio

Choose Tidio if you want affordable live chat, simple ticketing and an AI agent without heavy setup.

## Bottom Line

Gorgias is better for serious ecommerce support teams. Tidio is better for smaller teams starting with AI chat.
