## InVideo vs HeyGen — Which Should You Choose?

InVideo and HeyGen both create AI videos, but they produce different types of output.

## When To Choose InVideo

Choose InVideo if you want to turn scripts, prompts or blog posts into stock-footage social videos and explainers.

## When To Choose HeyGen

Choose HeyGen if you want AI avatars, voice cloning, sales videos, training videos or multilingual video translation.

## Bottom Line

InVideo is better for text-to-video marketing content. HeyGen is better for avatar-led video.
