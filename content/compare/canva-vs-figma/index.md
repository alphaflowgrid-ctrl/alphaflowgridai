## Canva vs Figma — Which Should You Choose?

These tools serve very different users. Canva is for fast, easy design by anyone; Figma is the professional standard for product and interface design.

## When To Choose Canva

Choose Canva if you need to create social media graphics, presentations, flyers or marketing content quickly — without design skills. Its template library and drag-and-drop interface make professional-looking results achievable in minutes. Perfect for marketers, small businesses and non-designers.

## When To Choose Figma

Choose Figma if you design digital products — apps, websites, interfaces. It is the industry standard for UI/UX, with powerful prototyping, design systems, real-time collaboration and developer handoff via Dev Mode. Professional design teams overwhelmingly choose Figma.

## Bottom Line

For quick marketing graphics, pick Canva. For professional product and interface design, pick Figma. They are not really competitors — many teams use both: Figma for the product, Canva for the marketing around it.
