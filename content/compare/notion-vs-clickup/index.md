## Notion vs ClickUp — Which Should You Choose?

Both are powerful all-in-one workspaces, but they pull in different directions: Notion is a flexible knowledge and documentation tool, while ClickUp is a structured project-management platform.

## When To Choose Notion

Choose Notion if your primary need is notes, wikis, documentation and flexible databases. It is the better tool for building a company knowledge base, personal second brain, or lightweight team workspace. Solopreneurs and small teams love its flexibility — you shape it to fit your workflow.

## When To Choose ClickUp

Choose ClickUp if you need serious project management — task tracking, sprints, time tracking, goals, workload views and reporting. It packs more native project-management features per dollar than almost any competitor, and its Unlimited plan at $7/user/month (annual) is excellent value for growing teams.

## Bottom Line

For knowledge, docs and flexibility, pick Notion. For structured team project management with reporting, pick ClickUp. Both have genuinely useful free tiers — try each before committing.
