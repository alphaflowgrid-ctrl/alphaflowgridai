## ChatGPT vs Claude — Which Should You Choose?

Both ChatGPT and Claude are excellent AI assistants. The right choice depends entirely on what you need them for.

## When To Choose ChatGPT

Choose ChatGPT if you need a versatile everyday assistant. The free tier is more generous, the plugin ecosystem is larger and image generation with DALL-E is built in. For most people doing everyday tasks — writing emails, summarising articles, brainstorming ideas — ChatGPT is the better choice.

## When To Choose Claude

Choose Claude if writing quality matters most to you. Claude consistently produces more natural, nuanced text that feels less robotic. For researchers, lawyers, writers and analysts who work with long documents, Claude's large context window and superior instruction-following make it the clear winner.

## Bottom Line

Try both free tiers before paying for either. Most users find ChatGPT sufficient for everyday use. Power writers and researchers will prefer Claude.
