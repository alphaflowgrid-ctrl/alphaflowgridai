## Ironclad vs LegalOn — Which Should You Choose?

Ironclad and LegalOn both serve legal teams, but they are not direct duplicates. Ironclad is a CLM platform. LegalOn is a contract review platform.

## When To Choose Ironclad

Choose Ironclad if your organization needs contract intake, approvals, collaboration, storage, workflow automation and reporting.

## When To Choose LegalOn

Choose LegalOn if your main pain is reviewing agreements quickly and consistently against playbooks.

## Bottom Line

Ironclad is better for end-to-end CLM. LegalOn is better for focused AI contract review.
