## Klaviyo vs Omnisend — Which Should You Choose?

Klaviyo and Omnisend both serve ecommerce email and SMS marketing. Klaviyo is deeper. Omnisend is easier and often more affordable.

## When To Choose Klaviyo

Choose Klaviyo if you need advanced segmentation, deep Shopify data and high-powered lifecycle flows.

## When To Choose Omnisend

Choose Omnisend if you want strong ecommerce email/SMS without Klaviyo-level complexity or cost.

## Bottom Line

Klaviyo is better for mature stores. Omnisend is better value for many growing stores.
