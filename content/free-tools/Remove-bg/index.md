## What Is Remove.bg?

Remove.bg is an AI-powered background removal tool. Users upload an image, and the tool automatically removes the background from portraits, products, logos, and visual assets.

## Honest Review

Remove.bg is excellent for one job: removing backgrounds quickly. It is much faster than manual editing and useful for ecommerce photos, thumbnails, presentations and social content.

The limitation is the free tier. Preview-quality outputs are useful, but high-resolution downloads and API usage require credits. For simple cutouts, it is very efficient.
