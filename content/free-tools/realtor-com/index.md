## What Is Realtor.com?

Realtor.com is a real estate listing portal for homes, rentals, property research and local market exploration. It helps buyers, renters and sellers browse available properties and compare neighborhoods.

## Honest Review

Realtor.com is a useful free property search tool. It is especially helpful for buyers and renters who want a clean way to browse listings and monitor inventory.

The limitation is that it is still a consumer portal. Agents and investors who need deeper comps, valuation modeling or CRM workflows will need professional tools.
