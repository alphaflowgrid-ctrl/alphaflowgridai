## What Is Canva AI?

Canva AI integrates multiple AI features into the popular design platform, including text-to-image generation, Magic Eraser, background removal, and AI-powered design suggestions.

## Honest Review

Canva's AI features are convenient for existing users and work well for quick social media graphics. Image generation quality is decent but not exceptional. The free plan is genuinely useful (5GB, 2M+ templates, ~50 AI credits/month), and Pro at $12.99/month ($120/yr) adds 500 AI credits, premium assets and Background Remover. Teams is $10/user/month (annual, 3-user minimum). The credit system can feel restrictive for heavy AI use — once you exhaust your monthly credits, more cost extra. Best value comes from using it as part of a broader Canva design workflow rather than as a standalone AI image generator.
