## What Is Semantic Scholar?

Semantic Scholar is a free AI-powered academic search engine from the Allen Institute for AI. It helps users discover papers, citations, authors, related work and research connections.

## Honest Review

Semantic Scholar is excellent for literature discovery. Its AI features help surface related papers and citations more intelligently than simple keyword search. It is especially useful for technical and scientific research.

The limitation is full-text availability. Like Google Scholar, it may point users to publisher pages where access depends on open access or institutional subscriptions.
