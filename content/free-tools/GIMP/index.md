## What Is GIMP?

GIMP is a free open-source image editing application. It supports photo retouching, graphic design, image composition, layers, masks, filters, plugins, and custom workflows.

## Honest Review

GIMP is a strong free alternative to paid image editors. It is powerful enough for many design and editing tasks, especially for users who do not want Adobe subscriptions.

The downside is interface polish. GIMP can feel less modern than Photoshop or Canva, and its learning curve is not beginner-friendly. For free image editing, it remains a reliable option.
