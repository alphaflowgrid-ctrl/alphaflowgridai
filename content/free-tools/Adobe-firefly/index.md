## What Is Adobe Firefly?

Adobe Firefly is Adobe’s generative AI platform for images and creative assets. It supports text-to-image generation, generative fill, text effects, image variations, and creative workflows connected to Adobe products.

## Honest Review

Adobe Firefly is a strong choice for commercial-safe creative generation. It is not always the most artistic AI image tool, but it fits well inside professional design workflows and Adobe’s broader ecosystem.

The limitation is credit-based usage. The free tier is useful for testing, but frequent generation may require Adobe subscriptions or extra credits. For designers already in Adobe tools, Firefly is very useful.
