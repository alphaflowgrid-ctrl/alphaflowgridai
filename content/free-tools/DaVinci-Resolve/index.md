## What Is DaVinci Resolve?

DaVinci Resolve is professional video editing software from Blackmagic Design. It includes editing, color grading, audio post-production, visual effects, motion graphics, and delivery tools in one application.

## Honest Review

DaVinci Resolve is the best free professional video editor for serious creators. Its color grading tools are industry-grade, and the free version is powerful enough for YouTube, courses, short films, and business video.

The trade-off is learning curve and hardware. Resolve is heavier than mobile editors and needs a capable computer. For quick TikTok editing, CapCut is easier. For professional editing, Resolve is excellent.
