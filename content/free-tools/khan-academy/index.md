## What Is Khan Academy?

Khan Academy is a free education platform offering videos, practice exercises, quizzes, learning paths, teacher tools, and test preparation across many subjects.

## Honest Review

Khan Academy is one of the best free education resources online. It is structured, trusted, and useful for students, parents and teachers. The math and test-prep content are especially strong.

The limitation is credentialing. Khan Academy helps people learn, but it does not replace formal courses, degrees, or professional certification. For free learning, it is excellent.
