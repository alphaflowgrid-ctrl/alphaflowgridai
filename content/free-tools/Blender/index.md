## What Is Blender?

Blender is a free open-source 3D creation suite. It supports modeling, sculpting, animation, rendering, simulation, compositing, video editing, motion tracking, and game asset creation.

## Honest Review

Blender is one of the most powerful free creative tools in the world. It can replace expensive 3D tools for many artists and indie game developers. The feature depth is incredible for a free application.

The trade-off is learning curve. Blender is not easy at first, and serious users need tutorials and practice. But once learned, it is production-grade and highly flexible.
