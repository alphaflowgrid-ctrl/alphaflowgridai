## What Is OBS Studio?

OBS Studio is free open-source software for live streaming and screen recording. It supports scenes, sources, audio mixing, capture cards, browser overlays, streaming platforms, and advanced recording settings.

## Honest Review

OBS Studio is the gold standard for free streaming and recording. It has no watermark, no recording limit, and no paid tier. For streamers and creators willing to learn the interface, it is unbeatable value.

The downside is learning curve. OBS is powerful but not beginner-simple. Users who want instant sharing or AI summaries may prefer Loom or Descript. For control and quality, OBS is excellent.
