## What Is Google Scholar?

Google Scholar is a free academic search engine for scholarly literature. It indexes research papers, books, theses, court opinions, patents, citations, and author profiles across many disciplines.

## Honest Review

Google Scholar is one of the best free starting points for serious research. It is fast, broad, and useful for finding citations and related papers. It works well for education, healthcare, legal research, and data-heavy writing.

The weakness is full-text access. Scholar often points to paid publisher pages, so users may still need institutional access, open-access copies, or library subscriptions.
