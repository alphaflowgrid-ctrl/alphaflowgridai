## What Is Socratic?

Socratic is a free Google learning app that helps students understand homework questions. Students can use text or a camera to get explanations, resources, and step-by-step learning support across subjects.

## Honest Review

Socratic is useful for quick learning help. It is simple, free and especially helpful when students are stuck on a concept. The visual search workflow makes it accessible for younger learners.

The limitation is depth. Socratic is not a full tutoring platform or curriculum. For structured learning, Khan Academy is stronger. For quick explanations, Socratic is valuable.
