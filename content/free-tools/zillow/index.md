## What Is Zillow?

Zillow is a real estate marketplace for property listings, rentals, home value estimates, saved searches and neighborhood research. It is widely used by consumers, agents and investors for real estate discovery.

## Honest Review

Zillow is one of the most useful free real estate research tools. Consumers can browse listings, check estimated values and monitor neighborhoods without paying.

The limitation is accuracy. Zestimates and public listing data are helpful but should not replace MLS data, appraisals or professional market analysis. Use Zillow as a starting point, not the final source of truth.
