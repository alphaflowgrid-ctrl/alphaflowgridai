## What Is Google Forms?

Google Forms is a free form and survey builder from Google. It helps users create forms, quizzes, registrations, feedback surveys and simple intake workflows, with responses stored in Google Forms or Google Sheets.

## Honest Review

Google Forms is one of the easiest free tools for collecting information. It is fast, familiar and works well for classrooms, surveys, event signups, hiring forms and simple customer feedback.

The limitation is advanced workflow depth. Forms that need payments, polished branding, advanced branching, HIPAA compliance or complex automations may need Typeform, Tally, Airtable or dedicated form software.

RadarScore reasoning: Value, ease of use and free-tier quality are excellent. Quality is strong for simple forms but limited for advanced workflows. Support is solid through Google documentation and Workspace admin resources.
