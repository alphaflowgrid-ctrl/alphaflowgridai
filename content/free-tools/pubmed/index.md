## What Is PubMed?

PubMed is a free biomedical literature database maintained by the National Library of Medicine. It helps users find medical research papers, abstracts, citations, clinical studies, and biomedical references.

## Honest Review

PubMed is essential for medical research. It is trusted, comprehensive, and free. Clinicians, researchers and healthcare writers use it to verify claims and find peer-reviewed biomedical literature.

The limitation is accessibility. PubMed is not written for beginners, and many records link to paywalled full text. For evidence search, it is excellent. For patient-friendly learning, other tools are easier.
