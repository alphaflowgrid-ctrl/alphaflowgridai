## What Is Have I Been Pwned?

Have I Been Pwned is a breach-checking service that lets users see whether their email address or password has appeared in known data breaches. It is widely used for security awareness and credential risk checks.

## Honest Review

Have I Been Pwned is simple, trusted and extremely useful. It gives users a fast way to understand whether their accounts may be exposed and encourages password changes, MFA and password manager adoption.

The limitation is scope. It does not fix breached accounts or replace identity protection. It tells you exposure status so you can take action.
