## What Is Runway?
Runway is the leading professional AI video generation platform, running on its Gen-4 and Gen-4.5 models. It is used by professional filmmakers, ad agencies, and content creators to generate, edit, and enhance video using AI. Note: the company rebranded from Runway ML to simply Runway.

## Honest Review
Runway produces the highest quality AI video available in 2026 for professional use. The free tier is misleading — 125 one-time credits is a sample, not a working plan. Pro at $35/month is the minimum for regular use. Note that unused credits expire monthly, so plan your usage accordingly. For content creators, Runway is worth every penny for the quality it delivers.
