## What Is Gemini?
Gemini is Google's flagship AI assistant, available across Google Search, Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Android devices. It runs on Google's Gemini 3.x model family and is the only major AI deeply embedded inside the Google productivity suite.

## Honest Review
Gemini's biggest strength is its Google integration — it can read your Gmail, summarise your Docs, and answer questions using live Search data. The free tier is one of the most generous available. If you live inside Google Workspace, Gemini Pro at $19.99/month is outstanding value.
