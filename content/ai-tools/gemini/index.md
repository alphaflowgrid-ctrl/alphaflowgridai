## What Is Gemini?

Gemini is Google's flagship AI assistant, available across Google Search, Gmail, Docs, Sheets, and Android devices. It runs on Google's Gemini 3.x model family and is the only major AI deeply embedded inside the Google productivity suite.

## Honest Review

Gemini's biggest strength is its Google integration — it can read your Gmail, summarise your Docs, and answer questions using live Search data. The free tier is one of the most generous available. In 2026 Google replaced the old single "Gemini Advanced" tier with a clearer ladder: a budget **Google AI Plus ($7.99/mo)**, the mainstream **Google AI Pro ($19.99/mo)**, and **Google AI Ultra ($99.99–$249.99/mo)**. If you live inside Google Workspace, Gemini Pro at $19.99/month is outstanding value.
