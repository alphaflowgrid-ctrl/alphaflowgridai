## What Is Diffit?

Diffit is an AI tool for teachers who need to adapt reading materials quickly. It can generate leveled passages, vocabulary, comprehension questions and printable resources from topics, text, URLs or uploaded content. Its main strength is making the same material accessible to students at different reading levels.

## Honest Review

Diffit is excellent at one specific job: differentiation. It helps teachers turn one source into multiple student-ready resources with adjusted reading level, language, length and comprehension support. The free Basic plan is genuinely useful because teachers can create readings, questions and vocabulary without paying.

The limitation is scope. Diffit is not a broad AI teaching platform like MagicSchool AI. It is strongest for leveled reading and comprehension resources, not full lesson planning, grading workflows or student tutoring. The Individual plan is reasonably priced for teachers who use differentiation weekly, while schools can choose a site license for wider access.
