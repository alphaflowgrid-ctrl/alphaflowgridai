## What Is Heidi Health?

Heidi Health is an AI medical scribe and clinical assistant. It helps clinicians create notes, summarize encounters, answer clinical questions with evidence and build documentation workflows. It offers a generous free plan and paid options for advanced templates, personalization, team workflows and practice-level support.

## Honest Review

Heidi Health stands out because its free plan is genuinely useful. Many medical scribe tools make clinicians pay before real testing, but Heidi gives unlimited AI documentation and clinical evidence features at no cost. That makes it one of the best first tools for clinicians exploring AI scribing.

The paid plans add serious workflow improvements, including advanced templates, patient-context answers, team features and EHR add-ons. The trade-off is that deeper practice workflows require paid tiers, and EHR integration may not be included at the lowest levels. For individual clinicians, Heidi is excellent. For practices needing full workflow automation, compare it with Freed, Nabla and Abridge.
