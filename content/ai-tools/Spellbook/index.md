## What Is Spellbook?

Spellbook is a legal AI assistant for contract drafting and review inside Microsoft Word. It helps lawyers generate clauses, review contract language, flag risks, compare terms to market standards and reuse playbook instructions. Spellbook is designed mainly for transactional legal work at law firms and in-house legal teams.

## Honest Review

Spellbook is strongest when the legal workflow already lives in Microsoft Word. That makes adoption easier for transactional lawyers because they do not need to move documents into a separate platform. Its drafting, review, Ask, benchmarking and Associate features are useful for contract-heavy work like NDAs, vendor agreements, service contracts and M&A documents.

The trade-off is scope. Spellbook is not a full legal operating system and is not built for litigation, matter management, billing or broad legal research. Pricing is also custom, so buyers need to start a trial or demo to understand cost. For teams focused on Word-based contract work, Spellbook is a strong fit. For teams needing complete contract lifecycle management, Ironclad or LegalOn may be better.
