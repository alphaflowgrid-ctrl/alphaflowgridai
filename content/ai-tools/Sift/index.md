## What Is Sift?

Sift is an AI-powered fraud prevention and digital trust platform for online businesses. It helps companies detect payment fraud, account takeover, fake accounts, promo abuse and other risky behavior. Sift uses a large global data network and machine learning models to score user actions in real time.

## Honest Review

Sift is a serious fraud prevention platform for companies where fraud directly affects revenue. Its strongest advantage is risk decisioning at scale: fraud teams can automate approvals, block suspicious activity and reduce manual review effort using workflows and risk scores. Reviews consistently praise its dashboards, chargeback reduction and analyst-friendly interface.

The downside is pricing transparency. Sift does not publish public pricing, and reviews note that cost can rise as transaction volume grows. Advanced tuning also requires fraud expertise, so it is not a plug-and-play tool for very small merchants. For fintech, marketplaces and high-volume e-commerce, Sift is powerful. For small Shopify stores, Stripe Radar or a simpler fraud app may be easier to justify.
