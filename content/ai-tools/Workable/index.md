## What Is Workable?

Workable is a recruiting and HR platform with an applicant tracking system, candidate sourcing, job board distribution, onboarding, time-off tracking and HRIS features. Its AI Recruiting Agent can source, screen and qualify candidates using a credit-based model.

## Honest Review

Workable is a practical hiring platform for SMBs and mid-market teams that want recruiting and HR workflows in one place. The ATS is user-friendly, job distribution is broad and the platform includes sourcing, interview kits, reports, onboarding and HR tools. The new Workable Agent adds a useful AI layer for handling candidates.

The main issue is cost structure. The base plans are clear, but add-ons can raise the real price quickly. Video interviews, texting, assessments, performance reviews and AI Agent credits may cost extra depending on the plan. Workable is strong if you want an all-in-one hiring system, but dedicated AI sourcing tools may be better if sourcing is the only bottleneck.
