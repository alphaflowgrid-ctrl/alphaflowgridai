## What Is Upstart?

Upstart is an AI lending platform and consumer-loan marketplace. It connects borrowers with banks and credit unions and uses AI-driven underwriting to evaluate creditworthiness beyond traditional credit-score signals. Its platform covers personal loans, auto refinance, HELOC and other lending products.

## Honest Review

Upstart is important because it shows how AI can expand access to credit. Its underwriting model considers more than a traditional FICO-only approach, which can help borrowers with limited or non-traditional credit histories get evaluated more fairly. For lending partners, the value is automated decisioning and broader approval potential.

The trade-off is borrower cost. Upstart-powered loans can still carry high APRs and origination fees depending on risk profile. For strong-credit borrowers, traditional banks or credit unions may offer cheaper terms. For lenders, Upstart is more of a partnership platform than a self-serve SaaS tool, so pricing and implementation are not transparent.
