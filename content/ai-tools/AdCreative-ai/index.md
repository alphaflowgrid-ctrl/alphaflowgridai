## What Is AdCreative.ai?

AdCreative.ai is an AI platform that generates ad images, copy, and social media creatives for Facebook, Instagram, Google, and LinkedIn campaigns. It includes a Creative Scoring AI that predicts which variations are likely to perform best before you spend money on ads.

## Honest Review

AdCreative.ai is best for agencies and e-commerce brands that need to test lots of ad variations quickly. The generation speed is impressive, and the Creative Scoring AI can help prioritize what to test. However, the output quality is inconsistent — many creatives look generic or need manual editing. The Starter plan is too limited for serious use, and the jump to Professional is steep. There is no free plan, only a 7-day trial. Customer support and credit rollover are common complaints in user reviews.
