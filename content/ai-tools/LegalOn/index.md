## What Is LegalOn?

LegalOn is an AI contract review platform for legal teams. It reviews contracts against attorney-built playbooks, flags risks, suggests redlines, answers legal questions, stores documents and supports contract workflows through a Word add-in and web platform.

## Honest Review

LegalOn is strongest when contract review consistency matters. Its playbook-based approach helps legal teams review NDAs, vendor agreements, customer contracts and procurement documents against defined internal standards. The platform is more structured than a general AI chatbot because it focuses on contract risk, clause review and redlining.

The main limitation is price. The official Individual plan is $550/month billed annually, which makes sense only if the user reviews enough contracts to save meaningful legal time. Teams and add-ons are custom-priced, so larger buyers need a quote. LegalOn is not a lightweight drafting assistant for casual users. It is best for in-house legal teams and contract-heavy organizations that need repeatable review quality.
