## What Is Harvey?

Harvey is an enterprise legal AI platform for law firms and in-house legal teams. It supports legal research, drafting, document analysis, contract intelligence, knowledge workflows, secure document storage and purpose-built legal agents for complex legal work.

## Honest Review

Harvey is one of the best-known enterprise legal AI platforms. Its strongest value is breadth: research, drafting, document review, contract intelligence and agentic workflows are designed for high-stakes legal and professional-services work. The customer list and positioning make it clear that Harvey is aimed at serious legal organizations, not casual users.

The downside is access and cost. Harvey does not publish pricing and generally routes buyers through sales. Reviews consistently frame it as enterprise-grade, which means smaller firms may find it hard to evaluate or justify. For large legal teams with significant document volume, Harvey can be compelling. For solos and small firms, Spellbook, LegalOn or even Claude may be more practical.
