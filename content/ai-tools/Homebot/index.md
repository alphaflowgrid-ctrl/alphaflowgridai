## What Is Homebot?

Homebot is a homeownership and client-engagement platform for loan officers and real estate agents. It sends personalized home finance insights, equity updates and buyer/seller signals to clients so professionals can stay top-of-mind and identify transaction opportunities.

## Honest Review

Homebot is strongest as a retention tool. It gives homeowners personalized reasons to engage with their agent or loan officer, such as home value, equity, refinance potential and buy/sell signals. That makes it more useful than generic email newsletters because the content is tied to the client’s largest financial asset.

The trade-off is database dependency. Homebot works best when an agent or loan officer already has a meaningful book of past clients. If you are brand new with very few contacts, the ROI may be weaker than lead-generation tools. For established professionals, Homebot can keep relationships warm and surface repeat/referral opportunities.
