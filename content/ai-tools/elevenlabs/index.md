## What Is ElevenLabs?

ElevenLabs is the leading AI voice platform offering text-to-speech, voice cloning, dubbing, and conversational AI agents. It produces the most natural-sounding AI voices available in 2026. The Creator plan at $22/month includes professional voice cloning — letting you create a custom voice from a sample recording.

## Honest Review

ElevenLabs produces the best AI voices on the market — there is no close second. The free tier gives approximately 10 minutes of audio per month (no commercial license), enough for testing but not production use. The $5 Starter plan unlocks a commercial license, but for creators the Creator plan at $22/month is the sweet spot with professional voice cloning and ~100 minutes of audio. Watch out for overage charges — they add up fast on the Pro plan if you exceed monthly limits.
