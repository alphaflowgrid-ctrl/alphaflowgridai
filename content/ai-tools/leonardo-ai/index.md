## What Is Leonardo.ai?

Leonardo.ai is an AI image and video generation platform built for creators, game developers, and designers. It offers multiple fine-tuned models, real-time canvas editing, custom model training, and a generous free tier that lets users generate images every day without paying.

## Honest Review

Leonardo.ai is one of the best Midjourney alternatives for users who want more control and a lower cost. The free tier gives 150 tokens per day, which is enough for casual experimentation and small projects. Paid plans are affordable, and the ability to train custom models makes it especially useful for game studios and artists who need consistent character or asset styles. Image quality is strong but not quite as photorealistic as Midjourney for some prompts. Support is community-heavy; enterprise users get dedicated help. It is a great pick for creators, indie game devs, and marketers who need a lot of AI art on a budget.
