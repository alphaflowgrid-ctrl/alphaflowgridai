## What Is Scenario?

Scenario is an AI asset-generation platform for game developers and creators. It lets teams generate images, train custom models on their own art style, create consistent assets and connect outputs into game pipelines through APIs and integrations.

## Honest Review

Scenario is most useful when consistency matters. Generic image tools can generate beautiful art, but they often struggle to match a specific game’s visual style across many assets. Scenario’s custom model training helps teams keep outputs aligned with their own art direction, characters and world design.

The trade-off is setup. Scenario works best when you have reference assets, a clear style direction and a production workflow. Casual users may prefer Leonardo.ai or Midjourney. Game teams, however, get more value from Scenario because it supports private generations, commercial licensing, API workflows, team workspaces and custom models.
