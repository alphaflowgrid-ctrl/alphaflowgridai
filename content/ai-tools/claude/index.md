## What Is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant built by Anthropic, designed to be helpful, honest, and safe. It excels at long-form writing, deep document analysis, coding, and nuanced conversations. Claude is widely regarded as the best AI for reading and summarising long documents thanks to its large context window.

## Honest Review

Claude consistently outperforms rivals on writing quality and instruction-following. The free tier is genuinely useful for daily tasks. Pro at $20/month ($17/mo billed annually) unlocks significantly higher usage limits — worth it if you write or research daily. The Max plans ($100 for 5x, $200 for 20x) are for power users who hit Pro limits regularly, and Team starts at $25/seat ($20 annual, 5-seat minimum).
