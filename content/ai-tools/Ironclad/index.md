## What Is Ironclad?

Ironclad is an enterprise contract lifecycle management platform for legal, sales, finance, procurement, HR and operations teams. It helps companies create contracts, automate approvals, collaborate on negotiations, store agreements in a searchable repository and use AI to extract contract data.

## Honest Review

Ironclad is one of the strongest CLM platforms for in-house legal teams that manage contracts at scale. Its biggest strength is workflow automation: teams can create repeatable approval flows, centralize contracts and reduce manual legal operations work. The AI features are useful for importing legacy agreements, tagging documents and surfacing contract data.

The trade-off is complexity and cost. Ironclad is not a simple legal AI drafting tool like Spellbook. It is a full CLM system, which means setup, workflow design, integrations and change management matter. Pricing is custom, and the official pricing page emphasizes quotes, products, partners and add-ons rather than self-serve plans. For enterprise legal teams, that can make sense. For solo lawyers or small firms, it is likely too heavy.
