## What Is Eightfold AI?

Eightfold AI is an enterprise talent intelligence platform for recruiting, talent management, internal mobility and workforce planning. It uses AI to match people to roles based on skills, potential and career trajectories rather than only résumé keywords. It is built for large organizations managing complex hiring and workforce transformation.

## Honest Review

Eightfold AI is powerful when hiring scale and workforce intelligence matter. Its biggest strength is the depth of its talent graph: the platform claims large-scale career and skills intelligence that helps recruiters match candidates, identify internal talent and plan workforce needs. For enterprises trying to move toward skills-based hiring, Eightfold is a serious option.

The downside is complexity. Eightfold is not a simple ATS that a small company can buy and configure in a day. Reviews consistently point to custom pricing, enterprise rollout time and implementation effort. It works best when a company has enough hiring volume, HR data and internal process maturity to justify the deployment.
