## What Is Midjourney?
Midjourney is the industry-leading AI image generator known for producing stunning artistic and photorealistic images from text prompts. It operates via a web interface and Discord bot. Midjourney shipped its first video model, V1 Video, in 2026 — available on all paid plans.

## Honest Review
Midjourney produces the best image quality available from any AI tool in 2026. The Standard plan at $30/month is the sweet spot for regular users, offering approximately 900 fast images plus unlimited Relax-mode generation. Note that Midjourney removed its free tier in late 2024 and it is not returning. All plans share the same image quality — you are paying for speed and volume, not features.
