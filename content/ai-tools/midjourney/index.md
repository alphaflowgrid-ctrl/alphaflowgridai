## What Is Midjourney?

Midjourney is the industry-leading AI image generator known for producing stunning artistic and photorealistic images from text prompts. It operates via a web interface and Discord bot. Midjourney shipped its first video model, V1 Video, in 2026 — available on all paid plans.

## Honest Review

Midjourney produces the best image quality available from any AI tool in 2026. The Standard plan at $30/month ($24 annual) is the sweet spot for regular users, offering approximately 900 fast images plus unlimited Relax-mode generation. Note that Midjourney removed its free tier in 2023 and it is not returning — the cheapest way in is the $10 Basic plan ($8 annual). All plans share the same image quality — you are paying for speed and volume, not features. Stealth Mode (private generations) is limited to Pro and Mega. *Its lower RadarScore reflects the lack of any free tier, which our methodology weights at 15% — the image quality itself is best-in-class.*
