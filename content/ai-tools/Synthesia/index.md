## What Is Synthesia?

Synthesia is an AI video platform that creates professional videos with realistic avatars from text scripts. It is widely used for corporate training, onboarding, marketing, and education videos in multiple languages. Users can choose from dozens of avatars, customize voices, and generate videos without cameras, actors, or studios.

## Honest Review

Synthesia is the leading enterprise AI avatar video platform. It turns text scripts into polished training, onboarding, and marketing videos without cameras or studios. Output quality is professional, but avatar-based videos can feel generic compared to real human presenters. It is best for organizations that need to produce video at scale. The free demo is limited, so most teams need a paid plan from day one.
