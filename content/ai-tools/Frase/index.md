## What Is Frase?

Frase is an AI-powered SEO research and writing tool. It analyzes the top-ranking pages for a keyword, extracts common questions, headings, and topics, and builds a content brief. It also includes an AI writer that drafts and optimizes articles inside the editor.

## Honest Review

Frase is one of the best value tools for research-driven SEO content. It saves hours by building detailed briefs and first drafts from SERP data. The 2026 pricing restructure made it more expensive than before, but it is still cheaper than Surfer SEO and Clearscope at scale. The AI writing is helpful but not exceptional — you still need a human editor. It is weaker than MarketMuse for deep topic modeling. Best for agencies and content teams that publish regularly.
