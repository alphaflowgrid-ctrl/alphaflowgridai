## What Is Eklipse?

Eklipse is an AI clip maker for streamers. It connects to Twitch, YouTube or Kick, watches stream VODs, detects highlight moments, and turns them into vertical clips for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels and X. It is built mainly for gaming creators who want content without manually scrubbing hours of footage.

## Honest Review

Eklipse is very useful for streamers who consistently create short-form content. Its biggest strength is automation: it can detect clutch plays, funny moments, chat spikes and hype moments, then format clips for vertical platforms. For creators without editors, that can save several hours per stream.

The free tier is strong enough to test the workflow, but serious creators will likely hit limits around watermarks, quality, storage and clip volume. Reviews are mostly positive around ease of use and time savings, but AI accuracy can vary by game and content style. Some clips still need manual review, trimming or rejection. Eklipse is best when you stream regularly and post clips several times per week.
