## What Is Freed AI?

Freed AI is an AI medical scribe built for clinicians who want to reduce charting time. It listens to patient visits, creates structured clinical notes, and lets clinicians edit or copy the final note into their EHR. Higher plans add EHR push, visit summaries, patient instructions, letters, referrals and coding support.

## Honest Review

Freed AI is one of the most practical AI scribes for solo clinicians and small practices. The biggest strength is ease of use: clinicians can start quickly, generate notes from real encounters, and customize templates without a heavy IT rollout. The Core plan is strong value because it unlocks unlimited note generation at a price many individual clinicians can justify.

The main trade-off is depth. Freed works well for common outpatient documentation, especially primary care and standard visits, but highly specialized clinicians may need more editing. EHR push is useful but not the same as deep native bidirectional EHR integration. Larger clinics may need the Groups plan for admin controls, onboarding and dedicated support.
