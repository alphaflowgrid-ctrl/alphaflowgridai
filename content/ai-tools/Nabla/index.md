## What Is Nabla?

Nabla is an ambient AI clinical assistant that helps clinicians generate documentation from patient conversations. It supports clinical notes, dictation, real-time intelligence and EHR-connected workflows. Nabla is used by healthcare organizations and clinicians who want to reduce charting time while keeping documentation accurate and structured.

## Honest Review

Nabla is one of the strongest AI medical scribes for clinicians who want fast, polished notes with minimal friction. Its strongest advantages are multilingual capability, broad clinical workflow support and EHR-focused deployment. The official site also highlights use across many healthcare organizations and clinician testimonials around time savings, reduced burnout and better documentation flow.

The main drawback is pricing transparency. Nabla does not publish a clear public pricing table, so buyers need to start a trial or contact the company. That makes it less convenient than Freed AI for solo clinicians who want to compare monthly costs immediately. For organizations that care more about EHR fit, documentation quality and deployment support, Nabla is still a serious contender.
