## What Is OpusClip?

OpusClip is an AI video clipping tool that turns long videos into short-form clips for TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts and LinkedIn. It analyzes speech, scenes, captions and framing to find highlight moments, then automatically creates vertical clips with subtitles and hook suggestions.

## Honest Review

OpusClip is one of the fastest ways to repurpose podcasts, interviews, webinars and long YouTube videos into short clips. The interface is simple, the AI captions are useful, and the automatic reframing saves a lot of manual editing time. It is especially strong for talking-head, interview and educational content where speech drives the story.

The main weakness is consistency. Not every generated clip is worth posting, and review feedback shows that some clips still need manual cleanup. Pure gaming footage, music-heavy videos and content where the best moments are visual rather than verbal can be hit or miss. The free tier is useful for testing, but serious creators will need Starter or Pro for watermark-free workflows and better export options.
