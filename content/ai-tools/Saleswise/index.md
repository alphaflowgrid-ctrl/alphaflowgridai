## What Is Saleswise?

Saleswise is an AI workspace for real estate agents. It combines CMA assistance, listing photo tools, flyer generation, listing descriptions, email scripts, social posts and other agent-focused AI workflows in one platform. The goal is to help agents prepare listings, pricing conversations and marketing assets faster.

## Honest Review

Saleswise is a practical AI tool because it is built around real listing workflows rather than generic chatbot prompts. The AI CMA assistant helps agents compare comps and prepare pricing conversations. Saleswise Studio adds virtual staging, decluttering and sky replacement, while the flyer maker and content tools help produce client-ready marketing materials.

The biggest advantage is simplicity. A flat $39/month price for unlimited access is easy for individual agents to understand, especially compared with tools that charge by credits. The trade-off is that AI-generated pricing, comps and marketing content still need agent review. It is also newer than long-established real estate platforms, so brokerages with strict MLS-native CMA workflows may still prefer their existing tools.
