## What Is Writesonic?

Writesonic is an AI marketing platform focused on AI search visibility, GEO tracking, SEO content, site audits and AI article generation. It tracks how brands appear across ChatGPT, Gemini and Google AI Overviews, then helps teams create content and fixes to improve visibility.

## Honest Review

Writesonic is no longer just a simple AI writing tool. Its 2026 positioning is much broader: AI search visibility, SEO audits, prompt tracking, AI articles and agentic workflows. That makes it more valuable for serious content teams, but less attractive for users who only want low-cost blog drafts.

The biggest strength is its GEO workflow — tracking how a brand appears inside AI answers is increasingly important. The downside is pricing. Starter at $79/month annually is not cheap, and the higher tiers jump quickly. The interface can also feel crowded because it now combines writing, SEO, AI visibility and workflow tools in one platform.
