## What Is InVideo?

InVideo is an AI video generator that converts text prompts into complete videos. It automatically selects stock footage, adds transitions, overlays text, and can even generate a voiceover. It is designed for marketers, educators, and creators who need to produce videos quickly without learning complex editing software.

## Honest Review

InVideo is one of the fastest ways to turn a blog post or script into a social-ready video. The free tier is useful for testing, but exports are watermarked and generation minutes are limited. The Plus and Max plans are affordable for active creators. Output quality is good for short social content and explainer videos, but it is not a replacement for professional editing. If you need AI avatars, Synthesia or HeyGen are better choices.
