## What Is Loom?

Loom is an async video messaging tool that records your screen, camera and voice into a shareable link. It is built for quick team communication, tutorials, bug reports, and feedback. It also includes AI features like auto summaries, transcripts, chapters, and filler-word removal on the Business + AI tier.

## Honest Review

Loom is the easiest tool for replacing unnecessary meetings with video messages. The free tier is generous enough for casual users, and the Business plan is affordable for teams. The biggest catch is that AI features are locked behind the Business + AI tier, which forces the whole workspace to upgrade. After the Atlassian acquisition, some users report performance hiccups and interface bloat, but the core recording and sharing experience remains fast and reliable.
