## What Is Darktrace?

Darktrace is an AI-native cybersecurity platform for detecting and responding to cyber threats across enterprise environments. It applies self-learning AI to network, email, cloud, identity, endpoint, OT and AI-security use cases, helping teams identify unusual behavior and respond before incidents escalate.

## Honest Review

Darktrace is strongest for organizations that need behavioral threat detection across complex environments. Its self-learning AI approach is useful because it can identify anomalies that traditional signature-based tools may miss. The platform also covers multiple security layers, including email, network, cloud, identity and operational technology.

The trade-off is cost and operational maturity. Darktrace is not built for small businesses looking for simple protection. Reviews mention premium pricing, tuning requirements and false positives during the learning period. Security teams also need enough expertise to interpret alerts and configure autonomous response carefully. For large enterprises and regulated industries, Darktrace can be powerful. For smaller companies, lighter EDR or managed security tools may be easier to justify.
