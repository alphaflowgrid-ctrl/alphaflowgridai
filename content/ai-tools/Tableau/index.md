## What Is Tableau?

Tableau is a business intelligence and data visualization platform for building dashboards, reports and interactive analytics. It helps analysts connect data sources, prepare data, create visual insights and share governed analytics across teams. Tableau now includes Pulse, Tableau Agent and Tableau Next for more AI-assisted analytics workflows.

## Honest Review

Tableau is one of the best tools for visual analytics. Its biggest strength is exploration: analysts can quickly build dashboards, drill into data and create polished visualizations that business users can understand. It is especially strong for teams that care about data storytelling, visual design and enterprise governance.

The trade-off is cost and complexity. Tableau is more expensive than Power BI for many organizations, and teams still need people who understand data modeling, dashboard design and deployment. The new Tableau Next and agentic analytics direction is promising, but the full value depends on Salesforce/Tableau ecosystem adoption. For serious analytics teams, Tableau remains excellent. For budget-focused Microsoft teams, Power BI is usually better value.
