## What Is AgentPulse?

AgentPulse is an AI video tool for real estate marketing. It turns listing photos into cinematic property videos with 3D-aware camera motion, aspect-ratio options, music and export-ready files for social media, MLS listings and ads.

## Honest Review

AgentPulse is useful because it solves a narrow but valuable real-estate marketing problem: turning static listing photos into video content. Most agents have photos but not time, budget or editing skills for video. AgentPulse fills that gap with a simple upload-and-export workflow.

The free tier is good for testing, but serious agents will need Standard or Advanced to remove watermarks, use more credits and export 1080p videos. It is not a full real estate platform like Saleswise, and it will not replace a professional cinematic shoot for luxury listings. But for everyday listing videos and social content, it is practical and affordable.
