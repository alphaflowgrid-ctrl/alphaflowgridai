## What Is Riverside?

Riverside is a remote recording platform that records audio and video locally on each participant's device, then uploads the files to the cloud. This gives you much better quality than Zoom or Meet. It also includes AI features like automatic transcription, Magic Clips, text-based editing, and speaker separation.

## Honest Review

Riverside is the best choice for remote podcasters and interviewers who care about sound quality. The free tier is enough for occasional recording, but the watermark and 720p limit push serious creators to Pro. The AI transcription and clip features are genuinely useful for repurposing long recordings into social content. It is not a full editing suite like Descript, but it integrates well with post-production tools.
