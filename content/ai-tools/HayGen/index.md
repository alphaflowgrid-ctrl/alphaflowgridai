## What Is HeyGen?

HeyGen is an AI video generator that creates talking-head videos with realistic avatars, voice cloning, and multilingual lip-sync translation. Users can type a script, pick an avatar, and generate a video in minutes without cameras or actors. It is popular for marketing, sales outreach, training, and localized content.

## Honest Review

HeyGen is one of the easiest avatar video tools to use. The free tier is limited to 3 videos per month, which is fine for testing but not for production. The Creator plan is affordable for solo creators, while Pro and Business are aimed at heavier users and teams. The credit system can be confusing — premium features like Avatar IV and video translation consume credits quickly. Overall, it is a strong choice for creators who need scalable avatar videos, but not for users who want real human authenticity.
