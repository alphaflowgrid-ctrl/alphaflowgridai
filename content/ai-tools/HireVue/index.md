## What Is HireVue?

HireVue is an AI-powered hiring platform for video interviews, assessments and recruiting automation. It helps companies screen candidates, run structured interviews, integrate with ATS systems and support high-volume hiring workflows. Candidates can complete interviews online without downloading an app.

## Honest Review

HireVue is strongest for large organizations that need to screen many candidates consistently. Its video interviewing, structured assessment and workflow automation features can save recruiter time, especially in high-volume hiring. The official site highlights ATS integrations, candidate flexibility, professional-services training, security certifications and 24/7 support in 20+ languages.

The trade-off is complexity. HireVue is not a lightweight hiring tool for small businesses. Pricing is quote-based, implementation can require planning, and reviews mention that configuration and candidate experience need careful management. For enterprise recruiting teams, the platform can be valuable. For smaller teams, Workable, Spark Hire or simpler ATS tools may be easier to adopt.
