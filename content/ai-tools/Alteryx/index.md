## What Is Alteryx?

Alteryx is an analytics automation platform for data preparation, blending, transformation, reporting and workflow automation. It helps analysts build repeatable data workflows without heavy coding, connect data sources, automate reporting processes and operationalize analytics across teams.

## Honest Review

Alteryx is powerful for teams stuck in spreadsheet-heavy or manual data-prep work. Its biggest strength is workflow automation: analysts can build drag-and-drop pipelines for cleaning, joining, aggregating and analyzing data, then reuse those workflows instead of repeating manual steps every week.

The trade-off is price. Starter begins at $250/user/month billed annually, and Professional or Enterprise pricing requires sales. That makes Alteryx a serious business investment, not a casual BI tool. It makes the most sense when the time saved from automation is measurable. For companies with recurring analytics workflows, finance operations, marketing reporting or governed data-prep needs, the ROI can be strong. For simple dashboards, Power BI or Tableau may be enough.
