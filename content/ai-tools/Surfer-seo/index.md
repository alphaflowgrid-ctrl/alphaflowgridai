## What Is Surfer SEO?

Surfer SEO is an on-page optimization tool that analyzes the top-ranking pages for a keyword and gives you a content score. It recommends word count, headings, keyword density, and related terms to help your article rank. It also includes an AI writer, content editor, and audit tools.

## Honest Review

Surfer SEO is the best tool for writers who want to optimize content based on real ranking data. The Content Editor is intuitive and the recommendations are actionable. However, it is expensive for solo bloggers, and the 2026 plan restructure pushed entry pricing higher. Credits do not rollover on monthly plans, and useful features like the SERP Analyzer and AI Tracker are paid add-ons. It is best for agencies and content teams that publish regularly.
