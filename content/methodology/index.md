## How We Score AI Tools

Every tool on AlphaFlowGridAI gets a **RadarScore out of 100**. That number is not a guess and it is not for sale. This page explains exactly how it is calculated, where our data comes from, and how often we re-check it — so you can judge our judgement.

---

## The Five Dimensions

Each tool is rated 1–10 on five dimensions. These are the same five shown in the "RadarScore breakdown" on every tool page.

| Dimension | What it measures | What a high score means |
|---|---|---|
| **Value for money** | Price vs. what you actually get, compared to direct competitors at the same tier | The tool delivers more capability per dollar than its rivals |
| **Ease of use** | Setup friction, learning curve, interface clarity | A non-technical user can be productive within minutes |
| **AI quality** | Accuracy, reasoning, output quality of the underlying models | Best-in-class results for the tool's primary use case |
| **Free tier quality** | How genuinely usable the free plan is (not a crippled demo) | Real work can be done without paying |
| **Support quality** | Documentation, response times, community, reliability | Help is fast, findable and effective when something breaks |

---

## How the Score Is Calculated

The five dimension scores (each 0–10) are combined into the final RadarScore (0–100) using the following weights:

| Dimension | Weight |
|---|---|
| AI quality | 30% |
| Value for money | 25% |
| Ease of use | 20% |
| Free tier quality | 15% |
| Support quality | 10% |

**Formula:**

```
RadarScore = (AI quality × 3.0)
           + (Value for money × 2.5)
           + (Ease of use × 2.0)
           + (Free tier quality × 1.5)
           + (Support quality × 1.0)
```

> Example — ChatGPT: (9×3.0) + (9×2.5) + (10×2.0) + (8×1.5) + (7×1.0) = 27 + 22.5 + 20 + 12 + 7 = 88.5, rounded to **88**.

Every published RadarScore is the direct output of this formula, rounded to the nearest whole number. There is no hidden adjustment — you can take any tool's five dimension scores, apply the weights above, and reproduce its RadarScore exactly.

## Where Our Data Comes From

For every tool, we draw on:

1. **Official sources first** — the vendor's own pricing and product pages are the primary source for all prices, tiers and feature claims.
2. **Hands-on testing** — we use each tool for its core use case before scoring AI quality and ease of use.
3. **Independent reviews & user feedback** — to sanity-check support quality and real-world reliability.

Every tool page lists its **sources** and a **"Last verified" date** at the bottom.

---

## How Often We Re-Check

AI tool pricing changes constantly. We re-verify each tool's pricing and key facts **at least monthly**, and immediately when we learn of a major change (a new tier, a price change, a rename). The "Last verified" date on each page tells you when we last confirmed its data.

## Our No-Paid-Placements Policy

- **We never sell rankings or RadarScores.** A higher score cannot be purchased.
- **Sponsored listings, when present, are always clearly labelled** as sponsored and do not affect a tool's RadarScore.
- **Affiliate links** may earn us a commission at no extra cost to you. They never influence scores or placement. We link to tools we would recommend regardless.

If you ever spot a number that looks wrong or out of date, [tell us](/contact/) — we read every message and correct errors fast.
